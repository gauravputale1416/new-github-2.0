`Welcome to Sipna C.O.E.T,Amravati` Sipna Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, established in the year 1995, aims to impart higher & technical education to the enthusiastic youths and envision them for a better tomorrow.

Since its inception, SSPM has been successfully running undergraduate and postgraduate courses in emerging areas through Sipna’s Arts, Science & Commerce college, Chikhaldara and Sipna College of Engineering & Technology, Amravati, affiliated to Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravati. We have already marched towards various Postgraduate, Management and Research Programs.
